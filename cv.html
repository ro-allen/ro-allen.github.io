Ro Allen

 

     

 

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Otago, New Zealand. My thesis uses high-throughput amplicon sequencing to investigate the responses of eukaryotic and prokaryotic marine microbes to ocean acidification. I am particularly interested in the fundamental dynamics of marine microbial communities, and the treatment of high throughput sequencing data.
